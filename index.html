Season & Training Tracks

Welcome to BattleHard Basketball. We offer competitive developmental tracks ranging from individual skill execution to local league play divisions (hosted at Elite Athletics in Batavia, Ohio) across elementary, middle school, and high school age brackets.

  • Individual Skill Development: 5-week intense focus on shooting, ball-handling, and decision-making.
  • League Play Track: Structured local gameplay and team development.
